College Experience in COE

Welcome to my Home page. I am a currently filling requirements to get into my major of computer science or CS. COE is a competitive bunch and if I don't try hard, I will struggle in the future. It also is a hard bunch of majors that need constant practice. The pre-requirements of COE majors are hard enough and are usually what causes many people to stray off and find a new major. CS is a hard major to finish through as there are many constant struggles that I go through when coding. Though, it was a rough start, I feel like I am knowing more and am able to think for myself when it comes to understanding my code. I am a currently a freshman in the CS major, so I should not lose hope and continuing pushing forward in my future years.
